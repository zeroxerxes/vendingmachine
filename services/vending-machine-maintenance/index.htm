Maintenance, Servicing & Repairs

Whether you choose to rent, lease or buy, with Best Vending Ltd, you can rest assured that your vending solutions will continue to operate smoothly for years to come. Our expert team will clean and maintain your machines to keep them in tip-top condition, completing any necessary repairs along the way. 

A fully managed vending service

Many companies sell vending machines – but not all of them operate and manage their machines as well. At Best Vending Ltd, we take great pride in offering a fully managed, fully operated vending machine service. This means that in addition to supplying your chosen machines, we will install, clean, service, maintain, repair and refill them for you. Essentially, we do everything we possibly can to allow you to benefit from having one of our machines, without you having to lift a finger. 

Vending machine servicing

When you choose to install a vending machine, you’re likely to have considered how big it is, what kind of products it can dispense and how easy it is to use. You might have been swayed by advanced features such as a full-colour media screen but it’s unlikely that you’ve delved any deeper into the inner workings of your chosen solution – and you shouldn’t have to. You’ve gone to the experts for a reason. At Best Vending, we come out to your site on a regular basis to service your machines. This includes frequent cleaning and checks to ensure that everything is running as it should be. You wouldn’t want your customers to go without because of a silly fault that could easily be resolved with the right expert knowledge. 

Vending machine maintenance

We source the best vending machines available on the market to guarantee that they will do the best job they can for as long as possible. Having said that, it’s still necessary to carry out regular maintenance to keep your machines running smoothly. For instance, it’s a good idea to clean your condenser coils once every few months to prevent any issues. We understand that you are busy and may not want to take care of this, which is why we send our expert team to do it for you. 

    Vending machine repair 

    In the unlikely event that something were to go wrong with one of your vending machines, Best Vending Ltd has your back. We aim to resolve all issues and queries as soon as they arise. Our response time to attend a call is 90% to prevent inconvenient downtime and to prevent any inconvenience to you and your staff – but with regular maintenance, this shouldn’t be too much of an issue anyway as we generally catch any problems before they cause trouble.
