Drinks Machines for Businesses: 5 Key Benefits of Hot Drink Machines for Businesses

December 16, 2024 | Time: | Location: | Category: news-header

“A cup of coffee a day keeps the doctor away”. Whilst this may not be completely true, according to the British Coffee Association, approximately 98 million cups of coffee are consumed daily. Clearly, there’s demand, and with good reason. Not just coffee, but tea and even hot chocolate boast several benefits that can help your workers to perform to the best of their ability.

With much of the UK workforce travelling to an office every day, it seems obvious that having drinks machines for businesses would benefit both employees and, in turn, benefit the overall goals of your business. From simply providing affordable refreshments, increasing productivity and offering convenience all the way to being an environmentally friendly solution – this blog will cover the five main benefits of having hot drinks vending machines in businesses.

The importance of drinks machines for businesses

For many people across the UK and the world, enjoying a hot drink in the morning is an example of ‘enjoying the little things’. Whether it’s a freezing cold morning and you’re enjoying a fresh hot chocolate or you’re craving a strong black coffee to help shrug off morning tiredness – a hot drink can help to get the day ticking along. The provision of hot drinks machines for offices allows employees this small delight whilst they work and is a great way to boost productivity and keep staff happy allowing them to recharge.

Don’t overlook the incentive aspect of drinks machines for businesses, ultimately, a hydrated and well-looked after workforce is one that is much more likely to work more efficiently towards meeting business goals. It’s not just for the benefit of the employees, but the wider business. The installation of a high-quality vending machine is an investment in the productivity of your staff.

Five key benefits that hot drinks machines provide:

But why should I invest in a hot drinks machine for my offices? What are the advantages of investing in a vending machine? To help answer these questions for you, we’ve compiled a list of the five key benefits that hot drinks machines provide.

Hot drinks machines provide affordable refreshments

If you do decide to invest in an office hot drinks machine for your company, you’re effectively providing your staff with free, or extremely cheap refreshments. In a country suffering a cost-of-living crisis amongst rising inflation, any way in which staff can save small amounts of money every day can make a big difference. This also helps the owners of companies, because, as we know, time is money. Circumventing the need to keep refreshing the stock of coffee, tea bags and hot chocolate sachets that seemingly run down on a weekly basis, removes extra hassle. Providing free or low-cost refreshments for employees benefits you and them, it’s a win-win.

Hot drinks machines for offices enhance productivity

A study by the tracking app DeskTime, found that coffee does indeed produce a spike in productivity for around two and a half hours after consumption, though this does vary from person to person. There’s actually science behind this, put simply, caffeine increases our ‘feel good’ neurotransmitters and blocks the ‘feel tired’ ones. Tea also contains a decent level of caffeine, so whilst it may not produce the same level of focus and alertness, it helps. Although hot chocolate doesn’t generally have caffeine, we can’t really put a price on the comfort it can provide, which helps to increase happiness and efficiency. Hot drinks machines are a small way in which you can work to increase staff productivity across the board.

Cost-effective

Despite the initial investment necessary to obtain a drinks machine for businesses, in the long-term it will turn out to be a cost-effective choice. If your company runs an in-house hot drinks service, café or catering company – you will save money as you’ll be purchasing supplies at cost. If you’re simply used to spending money every week on coffee, tea bags and bags of hot chocolate, you can skip these purchases that add up over time, with one initial investment.

Drinks machines for business: Convenient and time efficient

Having a hot drinks machine inside your premises will prove extremely convenient and time-efficient for employees. In the past, they may have had to leave the building entirely and walk to the nearest coffee shop or hot drinks stall. Now, they can stay on site and cut out that unnecessary travel time. This can help to combat employees spending time away from their desks when they’re not working. They can still benefit from occasional breaks to get up, move around and grab a cup of warm tea – they just don’t need to walk ten minutes to do so.

Hot drinks machines for offices are an environmentally friendly solution

This may come as a surprise to you, but hot drinks machines can be environmentally friendly investments in several ways. Many machines are designed to be energy-efficient, only using the power needed to heat water and keep it hot. Furthermore, some machines use coffee beans and tea leaves that are sustainably sourced, reducing the environmental impact of the tea and coffee production process. Not only this but some hot drinks machines are made from recyclable materials and can be easily disassembled for recycling at the end of their life.
