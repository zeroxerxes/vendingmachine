Office Hot Drinks Vending Machines: 5 Key Benefits of Office Coffee Machines

December 16, 2024 | Time: | Location: | Category: news-header

We probably don’t need to convince you that hot drinks machines at the office will be a huge hit. In fact, in a society fuelled primarily by coffee and tea, people have come to expect hot drinks in the workplace. But did you know that installing office hot drinks vending machines can benefit your business as well as boosting morale? There are multiple reasons to invest in coffee machines for your office. Here are our top five:

1. Caffeine!

Who hasn’t woken up and needed a good cup of coffee before they can function? The miraculous effects of caffeine are undeniable. Indeed, this stimulant helps us to stay alert throughout the working day, ensuring that we remain motivated and productive, as well as being prone to fewer mistakes. In fact, you could say that caffeine is an ally when it comes to health and safety!

Caffeine has also been shown to relieve both pain and stress, which are common in desk-based jobs. The happier and more comfortable your staff feels, the more productive they will be in their job, ultimately resulting in better output and profits for your business overall.

It’s not just about coffee, either. Beyond coffee vending machines for offices, you might like to think about the benefits of hot drinks machines overall. Indeed, tea also contains a powerful stimulant that’s equally capable of keeping us alert and awake.

2. Morale

One of the major benefits of hot drinks machines at the office is staff appreciation. When your workforce feels valued and appreciated, they are more likely to commit to your business and go above and beyond to drive impactful results for your brand. What’s more, your new hot drinks vending machine is the perfect place for office staff to come together and share a quick creative moment. You never know when the next big idea could strike!

3. Visitor experience

If your office often welcomes visitors and potential clients, having a good range of hot drinks and quality coffee on offer is essential. It’s amazing what a difference this small gesture can make to their perception of your products and services overall.

4. Time

The advantage of hot drinks vending machines over traditional methods for preparing teas and coffees – like the good old kettle – is that hot drinks machines save time. It may not seem significant that your employees take ten minutes out of their day to prepare their favourite hot drink, but when you add this time up over the course of the year and multiply it by the number of workers in your team, you might find yourself wasting a considerable chunk of time that could be otherwise spent turning a profit. With office hot drinks vending machines, it’s perfectly possible to prepare an exquisite hot drink to suit any taste simply at the touch of a button.

It’s not just a matter of the time it would take to prepare the drink, either. With hot drinks vending machines and coffee vending machines for offices, you can be sure that you already have the essential components for a good brew right there at your business. People won’t waste time leaving the office to go to the local supermarket for supplies or spend their entire breaktime in search of a local Starbucks – time that could otherwise be spent focussing on or discussing work.

5. Money

Of course, if people are less likely to leave your site in search of coffee, they are less likely to spend their money elsewhere. If you choose to charge for your office hot drinks, you will be able to turn a considerable profit over a relatively short period of time.

We highly recommend considering price carefully when it comes to your office hot drinks vending machines. Whilst charging for the privilege of office coffee may be a good idea at larger corporate firms with hundreds, if not thousands of employees and numerous visitors also keen to get their hands on a good brew, the profits you could make from your coffee vending machines for offices may be offset by a drop in morale. Allowing your staff to access your hot drinks machines for free, on the other hand, will ensure they remain positive and grateful, creating a more pleasant working environment overall.

Is it worth investing in hot drinks vending machines for offices?

As you can see, there are multiple ways in which your office could benefit from a hot drinks vending machine. The type of machine you will choose and the options you provide will ultimately depend on your unique business and its goals.
