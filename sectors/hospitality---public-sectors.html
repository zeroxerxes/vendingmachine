    The benefits of vending in the public sector

    We’ve all found ourselves in a situation where we’re waiting for a bus, train or even a doctor, with no access to food or drink. Public sector vending machines are therefore highly appreciated, especially if you can access the products inside them via card or contactless payment, since – particularly in emergency situations – it’s highly unlikely that you’ll be carrying cash. 

    Public sector vending machines can be placed in train stations, bus stations, hospital waiting rooms or any other public space. They’re particularly convenient as they can be accessed 24/7, ensuring that people can always get the products they want when they want them, without service providers having to operate café or other catering services outside normal working hours. This also helps institutions to save money and make profits at the same time, making vending machines not only convenient but also cost-effective.
